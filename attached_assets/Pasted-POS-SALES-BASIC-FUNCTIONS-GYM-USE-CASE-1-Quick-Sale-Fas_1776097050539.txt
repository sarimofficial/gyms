POS & SALES – BASIC FUNCTIONS (GYM USE CASE)
🔹 1. Quick Sale (Fast Billing)
Walk-in customer ya member dono ke liye
Simple flow:
Product select
Qty enter
Total auto calculate
Payment done

Use case:
Protein shake, water bottle, supplements

🔹 2. Member Linked Sale
Sale ko kisi member ke account se link karna
Member ko later charge ya history maintain

Example:
Member ne supplement liya → bill uske account mein add

🔹 3. Multi Item Cart System
Ek sale mein multiple products add karna
{
  "items": [
    { "product": "Whey Protein", "qty": 1 },
    { "product": "Water Bottle", "qty": 2 }
  ]
}
🔹 4. Auto Price Calculation
Qty × price = total
Discount apply
Final amount calculate
🔹 5. Discount System
Fixed discount (Rs 500)
Percentage discount (10%)
🔹 6. Payment Methods

Multiple options:

Cash
Card
JazzCash
Easypaisa
🔹 7. Paid / Unpaid / Partial Payment
Paid → full payment
Partial → kuch amount due
Unpaid → pura due
🔹 8. Due Tracking (VERY IMPORTANT)
Customer ka outstanding balance track karo
Member ke profile mein show karo
🔹 9. Receipt Generation
Sale ke baad receipt generate:
Date
Items
Total
Payment method
🔹 10. Inventory Auto Update
Sale hone par:
Stock reduce ho jaye
🔹 11. Low Stock Alert
Agar stock kam ho:
Alert show karo
🔹 12. Daily Sales Summary
Total sales today
Cash vs online breakdown
🔹 13. Sales History
Har sale ka record:
Date
Items
Amount
Customer
🔹 14. Return / Refund System
Product return
Amount adjust ya refund
🔹 15. Barcode / QR Scan (Optional but powerful)
Product scan → auto add to cart
📊 IMPORTANT API FUNCTIONS (POS)
Create Sale
POST /sales
Get All Sales
GET /sales
Get Single Sale
GET /sales/:id
Update Sale
PUT /sales/:id
Delete / Cancel Sale
DELETE /sales/:id
Get Member Sales
GET /members/:id/sales
Sales Summary
GET /sales/stats/summary
Low Stock Products
GET /products/low-stock/list
